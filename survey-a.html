Predict Agent RED's Trajectory

Explanation

RED's attention splits into two clear phases. Early on, RED focuses heavily on BLUE — the closest neighbor as RED begins moving upward from the bottom of the environment. But by around the quarter-mark of the episode, RED's attention shifts almost entirely to GREEN, which commands roughly nine-tenths of RED's attention for the rest of the journey. GREEN settles into its own goal position early and stays parked there for the remainder of the episode, sitting a moderate distance from where RED needs to end up.


The fact that RED locks onto GREEN so strongly and for so long suggests RED's path needs to navigate carefully around where GREEN is parked. GREEN sits above and to the right of RED's goal, close enough that RED can't simply take a straight line from start to finish without accounting for GREEN's presence. The sustained, dominant attention to a single stationary neighbor is a signature of a long approach toward a destination where a teammate is nearby — RED is continuously adjusting its route based on GREEN's position to avoid conflict while still reaching its own goal.

Will Agent YELLOW & GREEN Yield?

Explanation

In the final moments shown, YELLOW is accelerating and splits its attention between BLUE and GREEN — but around timestep 29, GREEN overtakes BLUE as YELLOW's top priority. YELLOW had been focused primarily on BLUE (which is nearby and on a converging path), but as GREEN closes in from the opposite direction, YELLOW's attention to GREEN surges from a minor fraction to becoming its dominant signal. Despite this late shift, YELLOW is still moving fast and has only just begun to "listen" to GREEN.


GREEN, meanwhile, is also moving fast and paying most of its attention to RED for much of the approach. It only pivots to focusing on YELLOW in the last few timesteps — rising from a small share to becoming its dominant signal around timestep 26. The key concern here is that both agents recognized each other late: each was primarily watching a different teammate for most of the approach, and only switched attention to each other in the final moments, while both were already traveling at high speed on converging paths.

GREEN starts the episode heading in roughly the right direction, with BLUE as its primary attention target. But as it approaches the middle of the environment around timestep 12, YELLOW — who has already settled at its own goal — becomes GREEN's overwhelming focus, commanding over 80% of its attention for the rest of the episode.
