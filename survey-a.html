Explanation

Around timestep 64, RED clears past YELLOW and approaches its own goal, which happens to be right next to where GREEN is parked — barely a gap between two robot bodies. RED's attention shifts sharply to GREEN, climbing from a small fraction to over two-thirds by the end of the episode, because GREEN is now the one it needs to avoid bumping into as it squeezes into its final position. In short, RED always listens most to whichever teammate poses the biggest collision risk at that moment.
